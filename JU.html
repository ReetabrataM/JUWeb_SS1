Welcome to Jadavpur University

Jadavpur University is at present an internationally recognized premier university of the country. It emerged from the National Council of Education, Bengal which was set up in 1906. In 1955, the Government of West Bengal, with the concurrence of the Govt. of India, established it as a unitary teaching University.
